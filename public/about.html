Introduction

Intelligent Networking for Media and Energy Systems Lab (or iMES), led by Professor Joohyung Lee, is with the Department of Software. iMES's research focus lies in designing/analyzing/implementing algorithmic and practical solutions of diverse networking problems for media and energy systems. Our mission is to significantly improve people's lives through our work devoted to intelligent networking system. Resent research interests include Future Media Streaming (e.g., Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality), Edge Computing, IoT platform, 5G networks, machine learning, smart grids (future power grids), and network economics.
